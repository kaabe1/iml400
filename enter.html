A Home: Not Paradise

An experience-based installation with AR technology that simulates a flight to Hawaiʻi.

Through the flight, the audience has opportunity to understand issues of colonialism, militarism & environmentalism through the lens of tourism. The experience is built to subvert the propagated image of “paradise” and understand the day-to-day issues that impact the local and Kānaka Maolī (Native Hawaiian) community.
